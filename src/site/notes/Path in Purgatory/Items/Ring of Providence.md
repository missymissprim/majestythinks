This ring is formed from black and silver bands of metal, spiraling around each other and wrapping around a single gem that seems to shine a different color at each facet.

This ring is imbued with 4 charges. While wearing this item, you can expend a charge to spend your reaction casting the [[bless\|bless]] spell (targeting only yourself) whenever you are subjected to an effect that requires you to make a saving throw. The spell requires concentration as normal and cannot be cast at higher levels by expending more charges.

At dawn, if this ring is missing any charges, it regains 1d4+1 charges.
